KUNAL DESAI M.S., MBA

PARTNER About Me

KUNAL DESAI M.S., MBA

KUNAL DESAI M.S., MBA
kdesai@biokosventures.com

Kunal Desai is deeply passionate about accelerating the pace at which life-saving therapies get to patients. In both his clinical and commercial careers, he has focused on addressing patients’ greatest clinical needs. He co-founded BioKos Ventures to serve this aim by building strong life sciences firms. After nearly a decade in ommercial roles in Big Pharma (Abbott Laboratories, Baxter), Kunal decided it was time to pursue his next intellectual challenge. He started medical school at the University of Illinois at the young age of 32 to pursue his life-long dream of becoming an oncologist. In industry, he spent his time understanding the commercial aspects of drug development; with a medical training he can now better appreciate the clinical aspects. When considering investments for BioKos’ portfolio, unal is able to draw on his commercial and clinical experiences to invest in assets that address high unmet needs while demonstrating strong commercial potential.

At the age of 24, Kunal was the youngest person to join Abbott’s post-MBA Management Development Program in its 30+-year history. He built a strong commercial foundation by working across functional reas in pharma and diagnostics: Sales, Market Research, Marketing, Business Development, M&A and Strategy. He continues to advise biotech lients, inventors & investors on commercial and M&A strategy. While working in Strategy for Baxter’s CMO arm, he learned the drug manufacturing process. A stint at Mayo Ventures provided practical VC experience and an appreciation of a highly effective technology transfer ffice. After a decade in industry, he has honed skills that lend well to spinning off and growing biotech companies: identifying blockbuster assets, reviewing pre-clinical data, product positioning, revenue forecasting, company valuation, term sheet negotiation and more.

Kunal received his MBA from the Indiana University Kelley School of Business with a focus on Finance & Strategy. While working full-time at Abbott, he completed an MS in Biotechnology from the University of Wisconsin – Madison. Not content to stop learning, he began an MD at the University of Illinois – Chicago in 2014. His intellectual journey began with a BS in Molecular Biology and BA in International Aid & Development from Indiana University – Bloomington.

ARKADIUSZ DUDEK MD, PHD

PARTNER About Me

ARKADIUSZ DUDEK MD, PHD

ARKADIUSZ DUDEK MD, PHD
adudek@biokosventures.com

Dr. Dudek has over 16 years of experience in the clinical management of cancer as a medical oncologist, over 17 years of cancer clinical research experience, and over 12 years in the fields of tumor angiogenesis, signal transduction, and cancer immunotherapy. He is currently a Professor and Medical Director, Oncology Clinical Trials at the University of Illinois, and also serves as Program Co-Leader, Cancer Targets and Therapeutics Program. Previously, he was an Associate Professor of Medicine and Director of the Clinical Trials Office in the Hematology, Oncology and Transplantation Division at the University of Minnesota.

Dr. Dudek’s clinical interests include phase I and II clinical trials in solid tumors, pancreatic cancer, malignant melanoma, kidney cancer, thoracic malignancies, and high dose interleukin-2 program. His laboratory research is focused on cancer microenvironment, angiogenesis, tumor vaccines and preclinical novel drug testing. He chairs and manages a broad range of clinical trials (from phase 1 through phase 3, from cooperative group, investigator-initiated, and industry sponsored studies) either therapeutic or non-therapeutic.

Dr. Dudek has worked closely with inventors to move discoveries through the pre-clinical process and develop clinical protocols for first-in-human clinical trials. He serves as a chief medical officer or advisor in several early-stage companies developing anti-cancer therapies. He has assisted with and sponsored multiple IND applications for novel oncology drugs, and has authored and co-authored more than 100 cancer related publications.​

Dr. Dudek received his M.D. from the Medical University of Warsaw, Poland in 1989. He completed his internal medicine residency in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, and received subspecialty training in Hematology, Oncology and Transplantation at the University of Minnesota. He received his Ph.D. from Military Medical Academy in Warsaw in 1998 where his PhD thesis was focused on hematopoiesis.

ARUN K. BHATIA

PARTNER About Me

ARUN K. BHATIA

ARUN K. BHATIA
abhatia@biokosventures.com

Through multi-faceted roles related to early-stage ventures, including intrapreneurial program leadership, research alliance management/deal structuring, venture diligence and investments, and pharmaceutical industry R&D strategy initiatives, Arun Bhatia has developed a deep passion for the focused design and strategic growth of new life-science ventures. He has utilized innovation principles, market research skills, marketing frameworks, and venture-based models to acutely define the value proposition, and build integrated business cases supporting program execution.

Arun served as Associate Director, Strategy & Commercial Development within the Innovations Strategy group at Baxter Healthcare based in Deerfield, IL. Within this role, he was responsible for systematically identifying novel opportunities, markets and early-stage technologies that were transformational to the company’s core areas. He was involved in analyzing industry shifts, competitive landscapes, market dynamics, and business models in order to fully assess the value and potential of organically- and inorganically-developed pharmaceutical and medical device technology opportunities.

In addition, Arun has worked in cross-functional teams to provide strategic direction and program leadership on internal/incubation programs. His early-stage technology development passion was further deepened with the launch of Baxter Ventures in which he worked to identify and move several opportunities through due diligence, one of which resulted in one of Baxter Ventures’ first investments in 2012.

Prior to Baxter, Arun served as Venture Development Director for the Illinois Technology Development Alliance (ITDA) where he screened and evaluated numerous life-science technologies and companies, and worked to develop the business model, marketing strategy and financial forecast elements of select clients’ business plans.

Arun received his MBA from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business with concentrations in Finance, Strategy and Entrepreneurship. Prior to this, he earned an MS in Biotechnology from the Illinois Institute of Technology and served in scientific academic research roles at Washington University-St. Louis and Northwestern University. He also has a BS in Biochemistry from the University of Rochester, NY.
